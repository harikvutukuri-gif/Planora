Our Story

PLANORA was founded by Thanishka Krishna Vootukuri , a Class 9 student from Samskruthi The School in Miryalaguda.

The idea behind PLANORA came from observing how students and busy individuals often struggle to balance studies, goals, routines, and deadlines. Instead of manually planning every hour, PLANORA uses AI to create smart and balanced schedules automatically.

Our mission is simple: help people manage time smarter, reduce stress, and improve productivity.
